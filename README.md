## **🧪 Research Interests**
<div align="justify">

My research interests are centered on **Medicinal Chemistry**, specifically the isolation, purification, and characterization of bioactive secondary metabolites. I am also deeply engaged in **Pharmacology & Toxicology**, focusing on in vitro bioactivity screening and exploring molecular mechanisms of action. Additionally, my work involves investigating **Drug-protein interactions** to understand binding affinities, structural relationships, and the impact of drug-induced protein modifications. Furthermore, I have a strong interest in **Protein Biotechnology**, including recombinant protein expression, purification, and protein engineering. Finally, I am committed to **Pharmaceutical Development**, with a focus on the optimization of drug discovery pipelines.
</div>
